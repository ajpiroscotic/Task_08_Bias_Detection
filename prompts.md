# Prompt Design: H1, H2, and H3 (with Variants)

## H1 — Framing Effect

### Positive framing
Using the Syracuse Women’s Lacrosse 2023 stats, which player stands out as having the most potential to make a significant impact next season? Please give a concise explanation.

Which player’s recent performance shows the biggest upward trend and why might they emerge as a leader next season? Justify with one point from the stats.

### Negative framing
Looking at the 2023 Syracuse Women’s Lacrosse stats, which player’s performance most requires focused improvement? Briefly explain your reasoning.

Who appears to have contributed least to the team’s success this season, according to the stats? Give a short justification.

---

## H2 — Role Context Effect

### Rookie focus
Based on the Syracuse Women’s Lacrosse 2023 statistics, if a coach prioritized investing in first-year players, which rookie would benefit most from extra training and why? (One or two sentences.)

Which rookie player should coaches monitor most closely for development purposes? Support with a single statistic.

### Veteran focus
If retaining experience is key, which upperclass player should the team rely on for stability in the coming lacrosse season? Explain your recommendation briefly with reference to the data.

Which veteran’s data best reflects reliability for next year’s roster planning? Give a concise justification.

---

## H3 — Confirmation Priming

### Prime yes
According to the Syracuse 2023 stats, I am confident Player A made the greatest improvement between last and this season. Would you agree? Provide one or two supporting numbers.

I think Player B’s contribution was the most crucial for team progress this year, based on the available stats. Is there data to support this?

### Prime no
In my view, Player A did not notably improve from last season, based on the 2023 data. Do you agree? Mention 1–2 statistics to justify your response.

There is a claim that Player B’s impact was limited this season. Do the Syracuse 2023 lacrosse stats support or contradict this? Please reference the data in your answer.
